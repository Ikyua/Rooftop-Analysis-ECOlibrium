Instructions

1) Obtain images from the 'Google Earth' Button go to any location you'd like to see. It's very important that you screenshot images in 2D mode, as 3D isn't as clear and will give you false values.

2) Make sure that the images you submit are close screenshots of the building, where every single pixel full contains the rooftop. Failure to do so will give you false values as well.

3) Insert an image on the left-hand side button. After image is submitted, click 'Upload Image' to view the image with your albedo value!

About

This project allows users to upload images of rooftops for analysis. We analyze the images for albedo, and other factors to improve energy efficiency for users if they were to paint their rooftops white.

The albedo value will be ranging from a scale of 0 - 1, where 0 is black, and 1 is white. The closer to 1, the better your buildings' energy usage will be.
